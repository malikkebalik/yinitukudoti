Andrew Garfield broke up with his girlfriend Alyssa Miller? (Update: no!)

July 2024 · 5 minute read

One of the most interesting subplots of the awards season was that… Andrew Garfield is incredibly popular and well-liked within the industry. I didn’t know that, honestly. I thought he was seen as a guy who was super-intense and more “unknown” in Hollywood. But that’s not the case! Andrew enjoyed his Oscar campaign for Tick Tick Boom, he enjoyed the interviews and he kept it very loose during the awards shows, showing up everywhere and making friends and posing for photos with Benedict Cumberbatch, Jessica Chastain, Jamie Dornan, Will Smith, Taika Waititi and everybody else. He went solo for the entire awards season and I didn’t even know if there was a girlfriend anywhere around. As it turns out, Andrew broke up with his girlfriend Alyssa Miller a few weeks before the Oscars?

Andrew Garfield and his girlfriend, Alyssa Miller, have reportedly broken up just one month after making their red carpet debut as a couple.

“They were together for quite some time before they went public, but work schedules mean it has been really hard to see each other,” a source claimed to The Sun Friday. “On top of that, it became clear that there were some differences between them, and it was decided they are better off apart, for now at least.”

The insider told the British newspaper that Garfield and Miller were a “really beautiful couple” and things were “going great at first,” but they just could not make it work in the end.

“It was disappointing for Andrew because he’d have loved to have shared the excitement of the awards season with someone he loved, but it just wasn’t supposed to be,” the source added.

The “Amazing Spider-Man” star is said to be doing well despite the circumstances and attempting to stay positive. “He is keeping his chin up and taking some time for himself after a hectic few months,” the source told the outlet.

Reps for Garfield and Miller did not immediately respond to Page Six’s request for comment.

Garfield, 38, and Miller, 32, took their love public while attending the 2022 SAG Awards together on Feb. 27.

Just days prior to their red carpet debut, the duo were seen hitting a tennis court in Malibu, where they showed plenty of PDA before Valentine’s Day.

“[They] were certainly happy in each other’s company and not hiding anything,” an eyewitness told E! News at the time.

[From Page Six]

Huh. Page Six says that they started up last November, so it wasn’t like they had been together for a serious amount of time. It was probably more like a fun relationship, just dating and it didn’t work out. I think it would probably be really fun to be single throughout an awards season? Like, just show up solo at events and hang out with everyone and dance and have a good time. I think that’s exactly what Andrew did.

Update: After all that, it seems that Alyssa and Andrew did not break up. Alyssa posted a photo on Instagram of herself and Andrew with the message “If you must gossip at least use a cute photo. Lol love you AG.”
