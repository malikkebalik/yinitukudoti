What Is the 'Book of Boba Fett' Star's Net Worth?

Temuera Morrison has held supporting roles in 'Star Wars' movies and games, but now he's headlining his own Disney+ series. What is the actor's net worth in 2022?

2 min read

The Book of Boba Fett premiered on Disney+ just ahead of the new year, and the Star Wars series is giving Temuera Morrison a chance to shine all over again. The actor joined the franchise by voicing Boba Fett in The Empire Strikes Back, and he went on to play the bounty hunter’s father in Attack of the Clones. Now he’s headlining his own series. So, what is Temuera Morrison’s net worth in 2022?

Temuera Morrison has a long history with ‘Star Wars’

Temuera Morrison has a long history with Boba Fett, kicking off his career by voicing the character and later playing the man who raised him. Following his involvement in The Empire Strikes Back and Attack of the Clones, he returned as Boba Fett in The Mandalorian Season 2. And the end of the second outing revealed he’d explore the character in more depth in his own Disney+ series, The Book of Boba Fett.

Of course, that’s not all Morrison has done over the years — in fact, it doesn’t even cover all the Star Wars projects he’s worked on. Not only did the actor voice Boba Fett in Star Wars: Visions in 2021, but he’s brought numerous characters to life in the franchise’s video games.

He’s appeared in other well-known films as well, Aquaman and Once Were Warriors two of the most notable among them. Morrison also voiced Chief Tui in Disney’s Moana.

With so many projects under his belt in addition to Star Wars, what is Temuera Morrison’s net worth today?

What is Temuera Morrison’s net worth in 2022?

It’s clear Temuera Morrison keeps busy with his acting career, but how does that translate to his net worth? Celebrity Net Worth places the actor at an estimated $5 million. That’s fairly average for a celebrity with Morrison’s experience — though acting isn’t the only thing he’s done to make a living.

Morrison has also done a bit of directing and producing during his career, and he’s even dabbled in music. There’s no shortage of skills the actor brings to the table. And his net worth is likely to increase if The Book of Boba Fett continues to be a success. After all, he stands to make more as a lead than in his previous supporting roles.

How does his net worth compare to other ‘Star Wars’ actors?

Temuera Morrison may have an estimated net worth of $5 million, but how does that compare to some of his Star Wars co-stars?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Morrison’s number isn’t far off from Ming-Na Wen’s. Wen plays Fennec Shand alongside Morrison in The Book of Boba Fett, and she also lands at an estimated $5 million.

Jumping over to The Mandalorian, Celebrity Net Worth estimates that Pedro Pascal is worth around $10 million. Now that he’s got his own series, Morrison could see a similar jump — though Pascal’s roles in hits like Wonder Woman 1984 and Game of Thrones likely contributed to his bottom line.

It’ll be interesting to see how the Disney+ series impacts Morrison’s fame over time. Could we see him in future Star Wars projects as well? Only time will tell, but Lucasfilm would be wise to take advantage of his talent.

The Book of Boba Fett is currently streaming on Disney+.
