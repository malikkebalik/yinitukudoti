Biography:

Alexis Knapp was born on July 31, 1989 in Avonmore, Pennsylvania, United States. Her first film appearance was in the 2009 movie Couples Retreat. The following year, she starred in Percy Jackson & the Olympians: The Lightning Thief.

She then appeared as Dylan in the television series Like Father, marking her small screen debut. She followed this with more appearances in other shows such as Family Guy, Super Fun Night and Ground Floor.

In 2012, she gained wider recognition as Stacie Conrad in Pitch Perfect. Other film roles include Alexis in Project X and Taylor Jaffe in So Undercover.
