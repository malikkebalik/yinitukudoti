It's official: Every 'Bachelor in Paradise' season 9 couple has split within days of the finale

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Redeem now

  • Spoilers ahead for the finale of "Bachelor in Paradise" season nine.
  • Aaron Bryant and Eliza Isichei were one of the strongest couples on "Bachelor in Paradise."
  • The two hit a few road blocks along the way, and have since broken up.

Aaron Bryant and Eliza Isichei formed a strong connection from the start of "Bachelor in Paradise" season nine.

However, trouble struck after costar Kat Izzo suggested to Eliza that Aaron had serious baggage, which was why Charity Lawson broke up with him on her season of "The Bachelorette." Charity's visit to the "Paradise" beach didn't exactly help Aaron's case, but Aaron and Eliza were ultimately able to work through their issues.

On Thursday's finale, which was reportedly filmed in June, Aaron and Eliza were one of only two "Paradise" couples this season to get engaged on the beach — the other being Kat Izzo and John Henry Spurlock, who was previously involved with Olivia Lewis.

But on Monday, Aaron confirmed that he and Eliza were no longer together. Here's what we know about their split.

Aaron took to Instagram to share news of his and Eliza's breakup

Just a few days after the finale aired, Aaron shared a photo of himself and Eliza to his Instagram stories, revealing the two had gone their separate ways.

"I want to thank our family, friends, and Bachelor Nation for all their support not just through the Paradise journey but after as well," Aaron wrote. "Tricky emotions watching it all unfold but unfortunately me and @elizaisichei's love story has come to an end."

He continued: "We're leading our amicable split with nothing but positivity and mutual respect for one another. Thank you all."

Aaron didn't give a reason for their split, and Eliza hasn't yet addressed the break up on her own social media. At the time of writing, both Aaron and Eliza still follow one another on Instagram.

The 'Bachelor in Paradise' finale hinted at their split, but it's not clear when Aaron and Eliza broke up

A montage at the end of Thursday's finale gave updates on the contestants from this season of "Paradise." According to the producers, Eliza and Aaron "hit some speed bumps" in their relationship. At the time the finale was edited, the two were apparently trying to work things out.

The couple's online interactions had also been sporadic at best from the time they got engaged in June until the split announcement in December.

In fact, the only photo of Eliza's that Aaron liked from the past year was posted October 26. Eliza, for her part, only liked three posts of Aaron's from the past year: one from August, featuring stills from his time on Charity's season of "The Bachelorette," and the other two relating to his time on "Bachelor in Paradise." (One was posted October 26, and the other November 10.)

Tellingly, neither Aaron B. or Eliza posted anything about their relationship or engagement once the finale aired, which also indicated that things had gone south for them.

Their break up announcement completed the trifecta of "Bachelor in Paradise" season nine splits. Kat and John Henry announced they'd ended their engagement earlier on Monday, and Kylee Russell and Aven Jones broke up due to "multiple infidelities."
