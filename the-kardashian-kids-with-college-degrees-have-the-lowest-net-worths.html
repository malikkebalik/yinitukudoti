The recent releases of the Forbes Magazine’s list of the wealthiest people in the world have been unraveling for the Kardashians. At one point, Forbes listed Kylie Jenner as the youngest billionaire in the world but the magazine later accused the star of faking her status. Kylie refuted the claims. 

This year, another member of the Kardashian family, Kanye West, has made the list of billionaires, and while Forbes lists him as worth $1.3 billion, Kanye claims he is worth much more. There’s no doubt the Kardashians have built themselves an empire over the years. However, the masterminds behind the accumulation of wealth are not the most educated in the family. And the most educated in the family are not the richest. 

Which Kardashian kids have college degrees?

Of all the Kardashian kids, only two have a college degree. The eldest daughter in the family, Kourtney, attended an all-girls catholic school called Mary Mount High School after which she shipped off to Dallas to attend South Methodist University. Two years later, she transferred to the University of Arizona and completed her degree in theatre arts and a minor in Spanish. 

The other Kardashian that boasts of a college degree is her brother, Rob Kardashian. Rob always wanted to be an academic, specifically a lawyer like his father. In 2009, he graduated from South Carolina’s Marshall School of Business, and three years later, according to The Atlantic, he sent out a tweet that he was going back to school for his law degree at USC’s Gould School of Law. 

However, the University quickly refuted the claims via a tweet but wished Rob the best at whichever law school he would be attending. Since then, there has been no news of Rob applying or pursuing his passion for law in any institution. 

What did Rob and Kourtney do after college? 

After completing her degree, Kourtney appeared in the 2005 series Filthy Rich: Cattle Drive and donated the money she earned to charity.

Rob set out to learn new skills after his degree. He appeared as a contestant on Dancing with the Stars along with his sister Kim Kardashian West. Rob emerged as the most improved dancer in the show going further than Kim and advanced to the finals together with his dance partner.

Rob also tried his hand at business in the subsequent years and worked with various brands like Rival Spot, PerfectSkin and BG5. He also worked on his successful sock line, Arthur George. In 2012, he appeared as one of the final judges of Miss USA 2012.  

Just how poor are Rob and Kourtney Kardashian? 

The most educated Kardashian duo is not the richest or most business savvy. While both of them are millionaires in their own right, their fortunes pale compared to that of other Kardashians.

Rob has the lowest net worth. He is valued at an estimated $10 million, with a large chunk coming from TV appearances and his sock line.

Kourtney has the second-lowest net worth at roughly $35 million. The richest of the Kardashian sisters is Kim Kardashian, valued at $350 million, according to Forbes, and Khloé Kardashian is valued at roughly $40 million.

In case you were wondering, their mother, Kris Jenner, is just as wealthy and has a net worth of $90 million.
